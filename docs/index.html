Privacy Policy

Fajr · Kasrah

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Fajr (“the App”), developed and offered by Kasrah, handles information when you use the App.

Local storage only

All user data — including habits, streaks, logs, and settings — is stored locally on your device only, using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

No collection, transmission, or sharing

No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party under any circumstances.

  • There is no analytics.
  • There is no crash reporting.
  • There is no tracking of any kind.

No accounts, servers, or cloud

The App does not use user accounts. There are no backend servers operated by Kasrah for the App’s core functionality, and no cloud storage of your habit data.

Subscriptions and payments

The only network activity the App performs related to payments is to process Fajr+ subscription purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Fajr never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information (such as full card numbers).

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete all your data at any time by going to Profile → Reset all data inside the App.
